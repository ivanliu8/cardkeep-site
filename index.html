CardKeep

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7 May 2026

CardKeep ("the App") is developed by Firemud Studio. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App accesses, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Data We Collect

CardKeep does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information you enter — gift card numbers, balances, transaction history, and card images — is stored exclusively on your device using a local database. No data is ever sent to our servers or any third party.

2. Camera Permission

The App requests access to your device camera solely for the following on-device features:

  • Barcode scanning – to read barcodes and QR codes on physical gift cards.
  • Text recognition (OCR) – to automatically read card numbers and expiry dates from a photo of your card.

Camera images are processed entirely on your device using Google ML Kit's on-device text recognition. No images or extracted text are uploaded anywhere.

3. Internet Access

The App may request internet access for two optional features:

  • Refresh store data – fetches publicly available gift card and store information from gcdb.com.au to keep the card type catalogue up to date.
  • Card images – downloads card artwork from gcdb.com.au for display purposes.

No personal data is sent during these requests.

4. Data Sharing & Export

The App includes an optional export feature that lets you create an encrypted backup file of your cards. This file is protected with AES-256 encryption and a password you choose. Sharing this file (e.g. via WhatsApp or AirDrop) is done entirely through your device's standard sharing mechanism. Firemud Studio has no access to this file or its contents.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following on-device SDKs, none of which transmit personal data:

  • Google ML Kit Text Recognition – on-device OCR
  • SQLite (via Drift) – local database storage

6. Children's Privacy

CardKeep is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
